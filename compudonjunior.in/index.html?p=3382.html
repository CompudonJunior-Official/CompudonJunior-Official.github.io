Global Digital Literacy Leadership Award

Mr David Saedi
Founder Certiport, Inc.
Chief Executive Officer at WinnowPro, California, USA

Mr. David Saedi, a distinguished advisor to national and global companies with technology-based educational programs and a true pioneer, par excellence in today’s digital age.

More than two decades ago, as the founder and CEO of Certiport, Inc. Mr Saedi pioneered the creation of Internet and Computing Core Certification, an innovation that has helped over 3 MILLION Individuals in over 100 countries. He created this groundbreaking initiative from scratch, marking a visionary leap in the digital landscape.
An entrepreneur with highly successful global track record across several industries, including Technology, Education, Hospitality and Media he also created other world-class certifications, including the Microsoft Office Specialist, and the Adobe Certified Associate.

Trailblazer of Digital Literacy Award

Mr. Sanjay Khanna
Past District Governor (PDG), Rotary International District 3011

PDG Sanjay Khanna’s legacy is one of enduring significance as he has not only left an indelible mark on society but continues to do so through’ his unwavering dedication. His commitment extends beyond just promoting Digital Literacy; it encompasses all aspects of community service. Throughout his illustrious career, he has made a profound impact on society by tirelessly supporting initiatives aimed at empowering youth with the essential skills needed to thrive in the 21st century.

His tireless efforts have paved the way for countless young individuals to develop the knowledge and capabilities necessary to succeed in an increasingly digital and competitive world. PDG Sanjay Khanna’s contributions are a testament to his enduring commitment to the betterment of society, leaving a lasting legacy that will continue to inspire future generations.
